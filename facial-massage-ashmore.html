Treatments available

Everything offered
at the Ashmore studio.

Four specialist treatments — each designed to work with the face at its deepest structural level. All techniques can be combined within a single session depending on your goals.

I.

Facial Sculpting

The complete protocol: buccal release, lymphatic drainage, and sculpting strokes combined. A natural face lift that produces visible results session one. No injectables, no downtime. The signature treatment at Soulful Sculpt.

Learn more →

II.

Buccal Massage

Intraoral facial sculpting that works from inside the mouth to release the masseter and pterygoids — the deep jaw muscles responsible for TMJ pain, clenching, and the heaviness that no external treatment reaches.

Learn more →

III.

Jaw Release & TMJ Massage

Targeted manual therapy for jaw pain, bruxism, clicking, and headaches. A structural approach to TMJ dysfunction — combining external massage with buccal intraoral technique for deep, lasting relief.

Learn more →

IV.

Facial Lymphatic Drainage

Manual lymphatic drainage to de-puff, sharpen contour, and brighten the complexion. Visible reduction in puffiness and improved facial definition — available as a standalone treatment or integrated into sculpting sessions.
