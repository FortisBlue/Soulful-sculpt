The method

Two outcomes.
One protocol.

Most courses teach you sculpting or they teach you pain relief. This workshop teaches you both — because the muscles that create jaw tension are the same muscles that define the jawline.

Understanding the face as a system — not just a surface — is what separates a practitioner who gets results from one who gives a pleasant experience. You'll leave with both the knowledge and the hands-on repetition to be confident from your very first session back in clinic.

I.

Therapeutic Release

Learn to work with the masseter, pterygoid, and temporalis muscles — the primary drivers of jaw tension, bruxism, and TMJ pain. Your clients carry years of stress in these muscles. You'll learn how to release it safely, methodically, and with immediate, felt results.

Buccal (intraoral) technique, myofascial release, cranial decompression. Everything that works from the inside out.

TMJ · Buccal · Myofascial

II.

Facial Sculpting

The same muscles that hold pain also define contour. When you release them, the face lifts, sharpens, and softens simultaneously. You'll learn the sculpting strokes, lymphatic sequencing, and lifting technique that produce visible results your clients will notice in the mirror the same day.

No injectables, no devices, no expensive products. Just skilled hands and a protocol that works.

Sculpting · Lymphatic · Lifting

What you'll learn

The full
curriculum.

A complete, reproducible treatment protocol you can offer from your first day back in clinic. Every module builds on the last — anatomy informs technique, technique informs outcome.

01

Anatomy of the Face & Jaw

The muscles, fascia, and nerve pathways that matter most. Why the jaw is the most undertreated structure in the body — and how to read tension patterns before you begin.

02

Buccal & Intraoral Technique

Working from inside the mouth to release the masseter and pterygoid at their deepest layers. Glove protocol, safety, client consent, and positioning. The technique that gets results no external massage can reach.

03

TMJ Release & Myofascial Work

Targeted release for bruxism, clenching, headaches, and restricted jaw opening. Techniques that deliver immediate, tangible relief — the kind clients message you about that evening.

04

Facial Sculpting & Lifting Strokes

The specific strokes, directions, and pressures that lift and define the jawline, cheekbones, and brow. How to work with gravity, bone structure, and individual anatomy for a result that looks natural — not pulled.

05

Lymphatic Drainage & Contouring

Reduce puffiness, sharpen contour, and accelerate results by integrating lymphatic drainage sequencing into the full protocol. The difference between a face that looks worked on and one that simply looks better.

06

The Nervous System & Facial Holding

Why the face stores emotional tension and how your touch can create genuine safety and release — not just physical relief. The understanding that separates transformative practitioners from technically competent ones.

07

Full Protocol & Supervised Practice

A complete, end-to-end treatment session — beginning to finish. Supervised practice with real-time feedback from Tenielle. You'll run the full protocol multiple times before you leave.

08

Business: Pricing, Positioning & Rebooking

How to talk about this service, what to charge, how to package it, and how to build a client base around it. What separates practitioners earning $350 a session from those charging $95.
