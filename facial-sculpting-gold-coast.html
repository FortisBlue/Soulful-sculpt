The technique

Not filler. Not Botox.
Your own anatomy, released.

Skincare works on the surface. Serums, peels, and light therapy can improve texture and tone — but the definition of a face lives in a deeper layer entirely. The sharpness of a jawline, the height of cheekbones, the lift at the outer eye — all of it is held in the muscles, fascia, and lymphatic system beneath the skin.

Facial sculpting works at that deeper layer. Using a combination of buccal (intraoral) massage, manual lymphatic drainage, and precision sculpting strokes, Tenielle releases the tension that compresses and flattens the face — and guides it back to its natural definition.

The Muscles

40+ facial muscles underlie the skin's surface. Where tension holds, definition is lost. Releasing the muscle restores the contour — and often relieves pain the client had stopped noticing.

The Fascia

Connective tissue that holds the face in its habitual shape. Myofascial release softens chronic holding patterns — the face restructures slowly, naturally, from within.

The Lymphatic

Puffiness is fluid that isn't draining. Manual lymphatic drainage redirects it immediately — reducing volume and sharpening contour in a way that's visible in the mirror the same day.

The Result

A face that looks more like itself. Sharper, lifted, more defined — not worked on. That's the difference between a surface treatment and one that understands the anatomy beneath it.

Two results

Most treatments make you choose: aesthetics or pain relief. Facial sculpting doesn't ask you to choose — because the muscles that hold tension are the same muscles that define your face. When you release them, both things happen.

Your session

What to expect at Soulful Sculpt.

Sessions run 60–90 minutes at the Ashmore studio. Every protocol is adapted to your specific tension patterns, anatomy, and goals. Sterile single-use gloves throughout the buccal phase.

01

Arrival & Assessment

Tenielle reads your face: where tension is holding, where lymph is pooling, what your anatomy is asking for. A treatment built specifically around you — not a generic protocol run on every client.

02

Neck & Jaw Preparation

Release of the neck, scalp, and external jaw muscles warms and opens the deeper structures. This also allows Tenielle to read how the jaw is holding before the more focused intraoral work begins.

03

Buccal Intraoral Release

Working from inside the mouth with sterile gloves, Tenielle releases the masseter and pterygoid muscles at their deepest point. This is the foundation of the sculpt — releasing what nothing external can reach. Learn more about buccal massage →

04

Lymphatic Drainage Sequence

Manual lymphatic drainage redirects puffiness, decompresses the face, and sharpens contour. The change from this step alone is usually visible immediately — the face looks less heavy and more defined.

05

Sculpting & Lifting Strokes

Precision strokes along the jawline, cheekbones, and brow structure complete the sculpt. The face lifts from beneath the skin, not across it — producing a result that looks natural, not pulled.

06

Aftercare & Planning

Personalised aftercare guidance and a recommended treatment schedule based on your tension patterns and goals. Tenielle will advise on how frequently to return for the change you're looking for.
